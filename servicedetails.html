Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property management services.

What is included in your property management fee? +
Our management fee includes tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, regular property inspections, 24/7 emergency support, monthly financial reporting, and legal compliance assistance. Additional services may include marketing, lease preparation, and tax documentation support depending on your chosen plan.
How do you handle maintenance and repairs? +
We have a network of vetted, licensed contractors for all types of repairs. For routine maintenance, we obtain multiple quotes and seek owner approval for expenses above a predetermined amount. For emergencies, we act immediately to protect your property and then inform you of the actions taken.
What is your tenant screening process? +
Our screening process includes credit checks, criminal background checks, employment verification, rental history verification, and income verification. We require tenants to have a credit score of at least 650, income 3 times the monthly rent, and no prior evictions or serious criminal history.
How often will I receive financial reports? +
You will receive detailed monthly financial statements by the 10th of each month. These reports include rent collected, expenses incurred, maintenance costs, and net income. All reports are accessible through our online owner portal 24/7, and we provide annual summaries for tax preparation.
Can I terminate the management agreement? +
Yes, our agreement includes a 30-day notice period for termination by either party. We ensure a smooth transition by providing all necessary documentation, tenant information, and property status reports to you or your new management company.
